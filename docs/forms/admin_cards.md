## Library card order help
(admin_cards)

![Image of a library card](https://docs.owwl.org/pub/Members/LibraryCardPurchase/igp_12c1d495ca3efbd448dd10e20801c643_OWWL-Library_Card.png)

OWWL Library System coordinates card/keytag orders twice a year--January and July. Prices of cards and keytags are dependent on quantity ordered. The combined quantity of members' individual orders will be used to determine cost. 

- Price range for cards: $0.22 - $0.84
- Price range for keytags: $0.125 - $0.75
- Price range for card/keytag combo: $0.33 - $0.99

Vendor requests Individual library orders of at least 250 please.
